About our ducks!

Since 2011 we here at Duck's have been serving our community by working on cars. When the owner
graduated high school he was lost and didnt know what to do. So he started fixing old cars as a way to pay rent. Like most fairytales this is where he found his love for helping people. That is what Duck's is all about.
Our mission statement is simple, help people get Ducked up before they go on a trip. So next time you are in need of your car getting brakes or an inspection, come on down to Duck's Garage!

We have state of the art garage filled with qualified mechanics that will do you and your car right!
